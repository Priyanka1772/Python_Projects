### Marketing Analysis
## Overview
* This project analyzes marketing campaign data to understand customer behavior, campaign effectiveness, and sales outcomes. * It explores customer demographics, product preferences, and engagement metrics to guide marketing strategies.

## Objectives
* Investigate which customer segments respond best to marketing efforts.
* Identify trends across product categories, marital status, and education levels.
* Explore relationships between income, age, and campaign acceptance.
* Support customer targeting through visual storytelling and segmentation.

## Tools & Technologies
* Python (Jupyter Notebook).
* Libraries: Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, Seaborn.
* Data preprocessing, visualization, and descriptive statistics.

## Key Highlights
* Cleaned and analyzed a marketing dataset to ensure consistency and accuracy.
* Explored acceptance rates for different campaigns and demographics.
* Visualized the impact of income, marital status, and education on campaign responses.
* Segmented customers based on responsiveness and spending behavior.

## Outcomes
* Clear identification of customer groups most likely to respond to campaigns.
* Insights into the effectiveness of each campaign over time.
* Recommendations for personalized marketing based on income and demographics.

## How to Access

* Download the file and open it Jupyter Notebook or any other software.
* Explore the charts, the tables and the queries.
